# Coffee Cake

## Ingredients
- 200g (1 cup) unsalted butter, softened
- 200g (1 cup) granulated sugar
- 3 large eggs
- 200g (1 2/3 cups) self-raising flour
- 2 tsp instant coffee or espresso powder, dissolved in 2 tbsp hot water
- 2–3 tbsp milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions
- Preheat and prepare tins: Heat oven to 180°C (160°C fan) / 350°F. Grease and line a 20cm (8-inch) round cake tin (or two smaller sandwich tins) with parchment paper.
- Make the coffee sponge: Beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well. Fold in the flour gradually, then stir in the dissolved coffee, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth 
- Add a topping/filling (optional): If using cinnamon streusel or nuts, sprinkle half the topping on the bottom of the tin, pour half the batter over, then repeat layering. For swirled cinnamon filling, mix sugar and cinnamon and swirl into the batter with a knife before baking 
- Bake: Pour the batter into prepared tins and bake for 20–35 minutes depending on tin size (use a skewer to check: it should come out clean). Larger or deeper tins may require longer baking 
- Cool and finish: Leave the cake to cool in the tin for 5–10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely. Decorate with coffee-flavored buttercream, glazed icing, or extra nuts if desired
